Integration with Drupal gives you full access to the OwnID feature set to simplify and streamline the login experience for your users. 

## How it Works

OwnID supports integration with Drupal through our pre-built Connector. To use it, create your OwnID app in the Console and select the _Drupal_ Connector as described below.
 <img src="/images/oi-tile-drup.png" width="200"  alt="Shopify Customer Accounts"></img>

OwnID supports integration with Drupal through our native pre-built Drupal Connector and the [Drupal module for OwnID](https://www.drupal.org/project/ownid).

<UserDataSnippet/>

## Installing the Drupal Module for OwnID
Download and install the OwnID module from the official [Drupal module page](https://www.drupal.org/project/ownid).

## Configuration Steps

<BuildAppSnippet connector_name="Drupal"/>

Copy the **appID** and **shared secret** from the OwnID app page into the settings of the Drupal for OwnID module.

Enjoy your Drupal-integrated passwordless authentication!</Check>

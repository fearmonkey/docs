## Installing The Connector
To install the OwnID connector, visit the Adobe Commerce [marketplace](https://commercemarketplace.adobe.com/ownid-connector.html).

## Configuration Steps
An OwnID application connects your Adobe Commerce website with the OwnID button in the front end. To create an OwnID application:
1. Open the [OwnID Console](https://console.ownid.com/) and create an account or log in to an existing account.
2. Create a **Adobe Commerce** integration and complete the onboarding.
3. Copy and paste the **appID** and **shared secret** into the settings of the OwnID Adobe Commerce connector.

OwnID supports integration with Drupal through our pre-built Drupal Connector and the [OwnID module for Drupal](https://www.drupal.org/project/ownid).

Configuration on the Drupal side happens through your Drupal Admin Dashboard. Ensure you have an account with admin access in Drupal.

## Installing the Module
To download and install the OwnID module, access the official [Drupal module page](https://www.drupal.org/project/ownid).
